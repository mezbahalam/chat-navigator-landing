Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "LinkedIn VIP Track" Chrome extension (the "Extension", "we", "our", or "us"), provided by Infolily, handles your information. Because we value your privacy, our policy is incredibly simple: we collect absolutely nothing.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Extension operates entirely within your local browser environment. It does not collect, record, process, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or message content to our servers or any third-party servers.

All data generated by the Extension (specifically, the list of LinkedIn profile handles you choose to mark as "VIP") is saved exclusively on your local machine using standard browser storage mechanisms (`chrome.storage` and `IndexedDB`).

2. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any other telemetry software within the Extension. We have no way of knowing how you use the Extension, how often you use it, or who you mark as a VIP.

3. Affiliation

The Extension is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to LinkedIn Corporation in any way. It interacts solely with the Document Object Model (DOM) of your active LinkedIn tab to inject visual highlights ("stars").

4. Third-Party Services

Because the Extension runs entirely locally, it does not share any data with third-party services. If you choose to contact us for support via email, we will only use your email address to respond to your inquiry and will not add it to any marketing lists without your explicit consent.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Because we do not collect your contact information, we cannot notify you individually of changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
